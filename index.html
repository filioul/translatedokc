Welcome to OKC's Translated Fiction Book Club!

We are an Oklahoma City-based book club that focuses on fiction in translation. Our meetings take place every 2nd Thursday at Bookish: Used Books & Art.

Follow us on Instagram
or join us on the Storygraph

Frequently Asked Questions

When and where does book club take place?

We meet every second Thursday at 7pm at Bookish in Oklahoma City.
1005 NW 36th Street
By Western, near Guestroom Records and Cock O' the Walk.

Do I need to attend every meeting?

Nope! Just come to any meeting. We don’t keep attendance. We appreciate your participation and your company, whether you only join us once or become a frequent member.

What if I don’t finish the book?

You’re welcome to join us whether you’ve finished our book pick or not, as long as you’re OK with spoilers being discussed.

Do I need to buy the books from Bookish?

You can join us no matter how you acquire the book.
